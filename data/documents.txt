# STANDARD OPERATING PROCEDURE: COLLISION AVOIDANCE (SOP-CA-2024)

## 1. Safety Zones
- **Green Zone (Safe)**: Walkways marked with yellow tape. Pedestrians have absolute priority. Forklifts prohibited unless stationary.
- **Red Zone (Hazard)**: Active loading bays and forklift main thoroughfares. Pedestrians must strictly follow crossing signals.
- **Shared Zone (Caution)**: Areas where paths cross. Max speed 5 km/h for all equipment.

## 2. Forklift Operations
- **Speed Limits**: 
  - Main Aisles: 10 km/h
  - Crossing/Shared Zones: 5 km/h
  - Near Pedestrians (< 5m): 3 km/h (Crawl Speed)
- **Right of Way**: Pedestrians always have right of way. Forklifts must yield immediately upon visual contact.
- **Load Stability**: Loads must not obstructing view. Unstable loads require a spotter.

## 3. Proximity Protocols
- **Zone A (Warning)**: Object/Person detected within 5 meters. Driver receives visual alert. Slow down to 5 km/h.
- **Zone B (Critical)**: Object/Person detected within 2 meters. Audible alarm triggers. Immediate stop required.
- **Zone C (Impact Imminent)**: < 1 meter or projected path collision < 2 seconds. Emergency Braking System (EBS) engagement authorized.

## 4. Pedestrian Rules
- **PPE**: High-visibility vests (Class 2 or 3) are mandatory at all times.
- **Distractions**: Use of mobile phones is strictly prohibited in Red and Shared zones.
- **Crossings**: Make eye contact with driver before crossing active aisles.

## 5. Incident Reporting
- **Near Miss**: Any Zone B activation without collision must be logged as a Near Miss.
- **Accident**: Any contact or Zone C activation requires immediate shift halt and investigation.
- **Safety Violation**: Loitering in Red Zone or Speeding (>10% over limit) triggers a safety citation.
