# Mission Security Guidelines
All operations must adhere to the strict protocol of "Observe, Report, Act".
Unauthorized access attempts should trigger an immediate lockdown of the affected sector.
Surveillance drones are to maintain a 50m altitude in urban environments.

# Threat Detection Protocols
Level 1 Threat: Suspicious movement. Action: Log and Track.
Level 2 Threat: Confirmed unauthorized person. Action: Alert Operator.
Level 3 Threat: Weapon detected. Action: Escalate to Law Enforcement / Security Team.
